Services

We aim to provide all our customers with a premium service, as well as with premium products. Whether you are a retailer or are responsible for a catering kitchen, we trust you will find our ordering and delivery service second to none.

Providing you place an order before noon on your delivery day, we can almost always offer a next-day service –to help avoid last-minute rushes, a member of our friendly telesales service will call you the day before your delivery day to find out what you need.
